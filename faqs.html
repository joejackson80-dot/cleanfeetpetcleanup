Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Omaha's #1 scooping service.

Do I need to be home when you clean?

No! Most of our customers are at work or running errands during their scheduled service. As long as we have access to your yard, we can get the job done and send you a photo proof when we're finished.

What happens if it rains or snows?

We work in almost all weather conditions, rain or shine. If conditions are dangerous (extreme lightning or heavy blizzard), we will reschedule for the next safest available day.

Do you offer one-time cleanups?

Yes! We offer one-time cleanups for special events, parties, or move-ins. Contact us for a custom quote based on the current state of your yard.

Is your deodorizer safe for my pets?

Absolutely. We use 100% pet-safe, non-toxic, and biodegradable deodorizers and disinfectants that are designed to be safe for paws and family members.
