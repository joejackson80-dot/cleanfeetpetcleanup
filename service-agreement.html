Service Agreement

By signing up for Clean Feet Pet Cleanup services, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

Access to Property

Client agrees to provide Clean Feet Pet Cleanup with safe and unobstructed access to the service area on scheduled service days. This includes ensuring gates are unlocked or codes are provided.

Aggressive Dogs

For the safety of our staff, aggressive dogs must be kept inside the home during the time of service. If we are unable to perform service due to an aggressive dog, a full service charge may still apply.

Billing and Payment

Service is billed monthly in advance. We require a valid credit card on file for recurring services. No long-term contracts are required; you may cancel at any time with 24-hour notice before your next scheduled visit.
