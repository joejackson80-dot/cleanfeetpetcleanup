Our Story

Clean Feet Pet Cleanup was founded with a simple mission: to help Omaha families spend more time enjoying their yards and less time cleaning them. We started as a small, neighborhood route and have grown into Omaha's #1 most trusted pet waste removal service.

We aren't a big national franchise. We are local people who care about our community, our neighbors, and their pets. Every yard we sweep is treated with the same care and attention to detail as our own backyards.
