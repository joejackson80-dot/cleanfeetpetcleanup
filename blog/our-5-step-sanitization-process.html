Our 5-Step Sanitization Process

We go beyond just "picking it up." Here's how we keep your yard truly clean.

The Clean Feet Standard

At Clean Feet Pet Cleanup, we pride ourselves on being Omaha's most thorough pet waste removal service. We don't just "scoop and run"—we follow a rigorous 5-step process designed to ensure your yard is safe, sanitized, and smell-free after every visit.

Step 1: Equipment Sanitization

Before we even step into your yard, we sanitize all our equipment, including our boots and rakes. We use an EPA-approved disinfectant that kills parvovirus, giardia, and other pathogens. This prevents the spread of diseases from one yard to another—a critical step many DIYers and budget services skip.

Step 2: The Systematic Perimeter Sweep

We don't just walk around and pick up what we see. Our technicians follow a "grid-style" perimeter sweep, walking in parallel lines to ensure no square foot of your property is missed. We check under bushes, behind sheds, and along fence lines where dogs love to hide their "business."

Step 3: Double-Bagging & Haul Away

All waste is double-bagged and removed from your property. We never leave waste in your personal trash cans, which avoids attracting flies and neighborhood pests. We transport it to a dedicated facility for proper disposal according to Omaha's environmental standards.

Step 4: Detail Spot-Check

After the main sweep, we perform a second high-detail pass on high-traffic areas like patio edges, walkways, and near back doors. This ensures that even the smallest traces are removed, providing the highest level of cleanliness for your family.

Step 5: Photo Proof & Gate Check

The job isn't done until we've verified your property is secure. We send a photo proof of completion to your phone and perform a double gate-check to ensure your pets stay safely inside your yard. You'll receive an email or text update before we've even left your neighborhood.
